Ball Thingamabobber™

Ever wanted a game to waste 5 minutes of your precious time? Well, you're in luck today. Meet the MARVELOUS-ish "Ball Thingamabobber™"

Created by a 16-year-old trying to learn Unity, it was made for no other reason, but to make the creator not to feel like a complete failure in programming.

Now here is the real description.

You play as a green ball called Thingamabobber, who is trying to collect all of his yellow cube friends and get to the shape party.

There are literally no obtacles (and there probably won't ever be any). Will you be able to help Thingamabobber get to the party that will start whenever he comes? Let sick rock be with you on this journey and good luck.

All the music is from Bensound
